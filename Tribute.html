Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela - Former President (South Africa)

Biography

Born: 18 July 1918.Mavezo, South Africa

Died:5 December 2013 (age 95 years),Houghton Estate,Johannesburg, South Africa

Spouse:Graca Machel (m. 1998 - 2013),Winnie Mandela(m.1958-1996), Evelyn Mase(m. 1944-1958)

Children:Zindziswa Mandela, Zenani Mandela etc

Education:University Of South africa (1989)

He was a South African anti-apartheid activist and politician who served as the first president of South African from 1994 to 1999.

He was the country's first black head of state and the first elected in a fully representative democratic election.

Achievements & Impact

He dismantled apattheid in South Africa, ushering in an era of democracy and human rights through his leadership and eventual presidency and fostering national reconciliation and spirit Of tolerance.
